About

More About Me.

I am Altyn Annataganova, a graphic designer with experience and a passion for creativity. I worked as a design engineer and soon realized that my true calling was visual expression and communication of ideas through design.

After graduating from the university, I started my professional career, starting with a job at a design institute, where I was able to develop my skills in designing houses, boiler rooms and building materials. This experience became the foundation for my future success.

In my portfolio you can see a variety of projects, logos, packaging, advertising materials, web design and much more. I skillfully work with various tools and software to create attractive and effective designs that leave a strong impression on the target audience. In addition to my professional activities, I actively participate in the graphic design community, where I share my knowledge and experience, as well as find inspiration from colleagues. I constantly strive to develop and keep abreast of the latest trends and innovations in the industry.
